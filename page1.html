Living at Burleigh there is always a multitude of things to do

Its just where to start thats hard


If the suns out the 1st thing you might want to do is go to the beach..:
To do so (please too dont forget your keys) ,go to the lifts and press G for ground level...
Exit the building past Club Burleigh and walk left up past the bowls club and cross at the lights to the beach..The walk is about 5 minutes.
___________________________________________

If you need milk or groceries ie a supermarket,there is a Woolworths within walking distance too.Once again,go to ground level and head left past the bowls club except walk (or drive) up Park st until you see the parking entrace to Woolies and travel down the lift to the store.It is about a 5 minute walk as well.
___________________________________________

If you need a larger shopping centre with a large variety of shops,the closest and best is Stocklands shopping centre on Reedy creek rd.Just by car ,follow park avenue up to west st,turn right and then turn left onto Reedy creek rd.It is about a 5 minute drive.
Pacific fair shopping centre is on the Gold coast highway about 10 to 15 minutes drive if you want a very large shopping centre.

Other Beachs
