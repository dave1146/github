Burleigh activitys


Theres many things to do in and around Burleigh without walking too far..

BURLEIGH ART AND CRAFT MARKETS

The Burleigh Art and Craft markets offer a great family day out on the last Sunday of every month, all year round. With over 150 stalls of locally made crafts, fresh produce, preserves, cookies and cakes there is something for everyone.  

JOHN LAWS PARK

John Laws Park offers one of the most magical views anywhere in the world! A stunning park right atop Burleigh Headland, there are views all the way to Surfers Paradise.  

JUSTINS PARK

Located right on the foreshore, Justins Park is a great location for kids and adults alike. There are many picnic tables and park bench seats aplenty. Make a day of it and bring a picnic, or grab some fish and chips and soak in the Burleigh atmosphere.

BURLEIGH HEAD NATIONAL PARK

Discover a wild, natural headland in the heart of the Gold Coast that offers walks along the rocky foreshore and through rainforest, and the chance to see whales in spring and sea-eagles soaring along the coast

THE VILLAGE MARKETS

The Village Markets (established in 2008) is a boutique fashion and lifestyle market trading every month, on the first and third Sunday at Burleigh Heads State School 8.30am to 1pm.
