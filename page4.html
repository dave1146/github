Main Control Panel

Making Coffee/adding milk 1st

Pull the container out and Fill the milk container with milk and replace.When done put the milk container in fridge to keep milk cool.(The colder the milk..the better the Froth).The knob allows you to adjust the froth or when prompted To Clean

Coffee Menu

Press the top button for quanity of coffee..Press the 2nd button with beans on it for taste.. Press the 1st 3 of the bottom buttons or the default machine made Cappachino ,Flat white or Latte

Unique self made coffee

Press the single cup button for a short coffee ,the double shot or the Long for amount of coffee.Then you can press the MILK button for milk.Press the button again to stop when you have the right amount.

Cleaning

When promted to clean,place a cup under the spout to grab the water.Or If the water spills over you can pull out the tray to empty but it has a big capacity.The machine self cleans itself so when you see message clean ,turn the knob on the milk container to clean.If it says empty beans container,pull the tray out and the beans container and empty.
